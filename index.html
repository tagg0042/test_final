Shipping Information

We only select healthy fish that we can ensure are healthy and free from disease. We use thick, durable, leak-proof plastic bags to hold the fish. We often use the double-bagging method for extra protection. We want to ensure that your new friend will have a safe travel and arrive to you in perfect condition. We fill the bag with clean, de-chlorinated water (about a third full) and add oxygen if possible. We seal the Bag: Remove excess air and tightly seal the bag with a rubber band or zip tie. If some how the fish arrives with illness or injury, your money back!
