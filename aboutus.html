About Us

Welcome to Simply Betta, your premier destination for all things aquatic! Founded in 2019, we are passionate about providing the highest quality fish and aquatic supplies to hobbyists and enthusiasts alike. At Simply Betta, we believe that every fish keeper deserves a vibrant and healthy aquatic environment. Our dedicated team has extensive knowledge and experience in freshwater and saltwater ecosystems, ensuring that we can assist you in selecting the perfect fish, plants, and equipment for your aquarium. We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and responsible sourcing. Our fish are ethically raised and sourced from reputable breeders, and we prioritize the health and well-being of all our aquatic life.

History

Simply Betta was founded in 2019 by Sus McKinney, who has always had a deep passion for aquatic life. What started as a small hobby in a home aquarium quickly blossomed into a thriving business as she recognized the growing interest in fish keeping within the community.

Initially located in LA, our first store was a modest space filled with a carefully curated selection of freshwater fish and supplies. As word spread and our customer base grew, we expanded our offerings to include a wider variety of species. This includes koi, double tail, velvet, and crown tail.

Local

We are a local shop! The staff at the shop have extensive knowledge about pets and can provide personalized advice based on your pet’s needs. Shopping locally supports small businesses and helps strengthen your community. Our shop offers specialty items that aren’t available in big-box stores, including locally made fish food and decor. Simply Betta provides educational resources, such as training classes and information on pet care. Local pet shops often prioritize quality, offering healthier food options and better-quality supplies. We support and encourage buying from and helping small businesses!
